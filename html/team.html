Our Team

Kirkendoll Management Team

The Kirkendoll Management team forms the financial, legal, and marketing backbone of our properties. We believe the devil is in the details, and numbers don’t lie — so we use sophisticated metrics to inform and support every strategy.

A lot of effort goes into creating the effortless elegance of a Kirkendoll club. Meet the people building our vision.

John Kirkendoll, Founder/CEO

John is the man with the plan.

A graduate of Harvard Business School, John is a 30-year entrepreneur and entertainment proprietor. His business acumen, finance background, and keen ability to spot new business opportunities have helped Kirkendoll Management take its place among the top entertainment management companies in the world.

John’s success allows him to support several charities doing valuable work in New Orleans and beyond. He’s also a licensed pilot, taking his Mitsubishi MU-2B-60 for a spin when his schedule permits.

Chuck Rolling, Chief Operating Officer

Chuck oversees Kirkendoll’s metrics-based approach.

Chuck joined Kirkendoll Management in 1992 as general manager. Since then, he’s traveled the country developing properties and supporting promising new ventures, from Myrtle Beach and Miami to Atlanta and Chicago.

During Chuck’s 20 years with the company, Kirkendoll has seen a substantial increase in profit. In 2011, he became COO, and continues to direct development from the company’s New Orleans headquarters.

Leigh Wright, Marketing Director

Leigh oversees the branding and marketing identities of each distinct Kirkendoll property.

An Alabama native, Leigh came to New Orleans in 2011. Before joining the Kirkendoll team, she dove into a career in Southern food and culture, becoming the head chef at Antoine’s Annex and writing monthly magazine columns on the South’s unique culinary history.

In her three-year tenure as marketing director of Kirkendoll Management, Leigh has championed the Kirkendoll brand across digital media channels and spearheaded the creation of six new brand identities. As the company’s online exposure has grown, its marketing overhead has shrunk — thanks in part to Leigh’s shrewd direction.

Tim Spratt, General Counsel

Tim’s legal knowledge informs Kirkendoll Management strategy, implementation, and compliance.

As the company’s top legal advisor for 4 years, Tim advises on a wide array of legal issues, including employment law, contract drafting and negotiation, governmental compliance, and risk management and mitigation. He oversees outside counsel, achieving consistency and quality in all of the company’s legal affairs.

Alex Chang, Chief Technology Officer

Alex’s tech and engineering expertise ensures Kirkendoll’s industry-leading online presence.

Alex has undergraduate education in Electrical Engineering from the University of California, Los Angeles and graduate-level training in management science for product development from Standford University. With over two decades of computer science and business intelligence experience, plus a decade of experience in product and systems development, Alex was a natural fit to direct the design and implementation of the latest technology into Kirkendoll’s club properties.

Alex’s accolades include building LowerMyBills.com’s credit card debt-matching engine to help facilitate is $350 million acquisition by Experian, and designing the most advanced biometric security systems for the Department of Defense. He has the rare ability to parlay his engineering skills into value creation on both the business and consumer levels.

Nicey Simoneaux, Executive Assistant

Nicey’s foresight and administrative support sustain Kirkendoll’s office operations.

Planning and organization are key elements of every successful office, and this is where Nicey excels. With years of experience in accounting, financial insurance, and other professional arenas, she motivates the Kirkendoll Management team to offer our best work on a daily basis.
